ABOUT TORA CONSTRUCTIONS

Building Excellence Across Upper Assam

Trusted Construction and Development Services Under the Leadership of Ashok Neog

Tora Constructions is the construction and project execution division of Tora Developers, a trusted name in land development and plotting projects across Dibrugarh and Upper Assam.

Founded and led by Ashok Neog, Founder and CEO of Tora Developers, Tora Constructions was created to bring the same trust into residential construction, commercial construction, architecture, interior design, and complete turnkey project execution.

With over 8 years of industry experience and 10 plus completed projects, the company has delivered work across Dibrugarh, Chabua, Dhemaji, Tinsukia, Sivasagar, and nearby regions.

Every project is handled with a focus on structural quality, transparent project management, practical planning, and lasting value.

From family homes and commercial buildings to office spaces and complete turnkey developments, Tora Constructions aims to deliver reliable spaces built with professionalism and care. Along with construction, planning, interiors, and turnkey execution, the group also supports clients with land and property selling services through Tora Developers.
