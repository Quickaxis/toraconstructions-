About Tora Constructions

Building Excellence Across Assam

Trusted Construction & Development Services Across Upper Assam

Under the leadership of Ashok Neog

Tora Constructions is a Dibrugarh-based construction and development company serving clients across Upper Assam. Under the leadership of Ashok Neog, we specialize in residential construction, commercial construction, architectural planning, interior design, and turnkey project execution.

With more than 6 years of experience and 10+ completed projects, we help homeowners, businesses, and property developers transform ideas into durable, high-quality spaces. Our projects span Dibrugarh, Chabua, Borboruah, Tinsukia, Sivasagar, and nearby regions.

From planning and design to construction and finishing, we provide complete project solutions with a focus on quality workmanship, transparency, and timely delivery.
